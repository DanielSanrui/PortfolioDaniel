About Me

I am a frontend developer passionate about creating dynamic, engaging, and functional web experiences. My expertise lies in working with HTML, CSS, SASS, and JavaScript, and I enjoy bringing projects to life by combining design and functionality.

In addition to my frontend skills, I have basic knowledge of backend development using Java. This gives me a more comprehensive view of projects and allows me to collaborate effectively with multidisciplinary teams.

My interest in design has led me to learn tools like Figma, which I use to prototype and transform ideas into clear and intuitive interfaces. I'm also proficient with essential technologies and tools such as VS Code, Bootstrap, Git, GitHub, and I even have some experience with WordPress.

I enjoy working in a team and believe in the importance of collaboration to achieve the best results. If you're interested in collaborating or just want to talk about web development, feel free to contact me via LinkedIn, GitHub, Twitter, or any other platform you prefer.
